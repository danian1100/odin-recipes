Onigiri

Description

Japanese Rice Balls. Fast and easy to make.

Ingredients:

  • 4 Cups of Rice
  • 5 Cups of Water
  • Quarter of a teaspoon of Salt
  • Quarte of Bonito shavings
  • 2 Sheets of Nori
  • 2 tablespoon of Sesame seeds

Steps:

  1. Make rice;
  2. Make 8 equal balls of rice;
  3. Fill the rice balls with bonito flakes;
  4. Give them a triangular shape;
  5. Add nori and sesame seeds.
